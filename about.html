Our Nocturnal Story

Why "Bat Bakery"?

Because bats are the unsung heroes of the nightâ€”just like our bakers. Founded by two night-owl sisters in Johannesburg, we cater to those who thrive when the sun goes down.

Our journey began in a small apartment kitchen, where we baked through the night to satisfy our own midnight cravings. Word spread quickly among fellow night owls, and soon we were supplying treats to local coffee shops that stayed open late.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients sourced from local suppliers
  • Freshly baked goods every evening
  • 50% of our menu is vegan or gluten-free
  • Compostable packaging and zero food waste
  • A welcoming space for all creatures of the night

Our Mission

To prove the best things happen after dark, one delicious bite at a time. We believe the night holds magic that daylight can't match, and we're here to fuel your nocturnal adventures with treats that are worth staying up for.
