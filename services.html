Themed Baking Workshops

Join our nocturnal baking community and learn to create spooky treats under the moonlight

Gothic Cupcake Decorating

Beginner Family Friendly

Learn to create stunning gothic-inspired cupcakes with our expert bakers. Perfect for Halloween or just because!

We'll cover black frosting techniques, edible glitter applications, and how to create miniature fondant bats and spiders.

When: Every Friday at 8 PM

Duration: 2 hours

Price: R250 per person (includes 6 cupcakes to decorate)

Skill Level: Beginner friendly

Age: 10+ (under 16 with adult)

Book Now

Vampire Bread Making

Intermediate Savory

Master the art of artisan bread making with a gothic twist. We'll teach you how to make our famous Vampire Garlic Bread and other savory night-time treats.

This workshop includes all ingredients and you'll take home two loaves of your own creation.

When: Every Saturday at 7 PM

Duration: 3 hours

Price: R300 per person

Skill Level: Some experience helpful

Take Home: 2 fresh bread loaves
